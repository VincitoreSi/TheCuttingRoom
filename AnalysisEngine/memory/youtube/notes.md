# YouTube Shorts — craft notes

- Shorts often repurpose long-form footage: expect a strong verbal hook plus a lower-third or
  centered caption. Retention leans on a question/loop that pays off at the end.
- Slightly longer tolerance than IG (up to ~60s); segment generously and keep `pacing` honest —
  Shorts can hold a shot longer than a fast IG edit.
- Audio is frequently original voiceover/commentary; when a music bed exists it is often a
  royalty-free track rather than an IG-style licensed trending sound. Set `audio_strategy`
  accordingly and prefer `substitute_equivalent` when the bed is generic.
- 9:16, 1080x1920 is the default; confirm from the frame.
- Capture the end-screen CTA ("subscribe", "watch the full video") in `virality_formula.cta`.
